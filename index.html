Inter School Youth Swimming Championship

In the recently concluded Aquatic Phenomenon – Hyderabad Inter School Youth Swimming Championship, the water babies of Tatva plunged into the pool with the certainty and confidence that comes from training long hours and resurfaced with a handful of medals in both the individual and team events. Our swimming contingent comprised 16 members this time. The competition was tough and aggressive and only the best ones survived.
Soujanya Singh – 2 Gold Medals in 30 m Freestyle Relay and 30 m Medley Relay
Taneeru Manikanta – 2 Bronze Medals in 30m Freestyle and 30m Freestyle Relay
Yogita – 1 Silver and 1 Bronze Silver in 30m Medley Relay and Bronze in 30m Freestyle
Roshan Lal – 1 Bronze Bronze in 30 m Freestyle
Tridev – 1 Silver and 3 Bronze Silver in 30 m Medley Relay, 3 Bronzes in 30m Butterfly, Freestyle Relay and Medley Relay

Telangana State Girls Chess Championship, 2019

Pujitaa Mynam of Grade III has won the hearts of the Tatva family. Congratulations! Our little girl has been ranked VII in the Telangana State Girls Chess Championship, 2019 in the Under 9 Category. Really proud of you, Pujitaa. Wishing you the best in your journey to scale greater heights and bring in National and International laurels. Thank you, parents, for extending your support and helping her achieve these milestones and victories.

All India Roller Relay Skating Competition

Shannvi of Pre-Primary II wins six gold medals at the 30th All India Roller Relay Skating Competition held in Goa.
She was awarded three gold medals in the Quad Roller Skating competition and another three gold medals in the Inline Skating competition. Thanks to the coach, Mr. Macha Giri of Tatva and her parents.
Shannvi – 6 Gold Medals
3 Gold Medals in the Quad Roller Skating competition
3 Gold Medals in the Inline Skating competition
